Bio

I am Jiajing Huang, and I also go by Horatio Huang. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Data Science, Analytics and Engineering at Arizona State University. I have over 5 years' experience in machine learning, deep learning, statistics and biostatistics with data analysis and modeling, and statistical inferences. My current research directions include the applications of informatic framework, causal learning (e.g. causal inferences, causal modeling, causal reasoning), and developments of machine learning algorithms/big data tools, and my research projects are primarily sponsored by National Science Foundation and U.S. Department of Energy.
